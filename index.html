PoseSense

A web-based pose feedback system that helps users practice movement, improve posture, and stay active through real-time interaction.


The Challenge

Many people want to move more throughout the day but lack feedback on whether they are performing poses correctly. Without guidance, posture mistakes can reduce effectiveness and lead to discomfort.

PoseSense provides instant pose recognition and feedback using only a webcam, making movement practice simple, accessible, and engaging.

Who Benefits

✓ Beginners learning proper movement
✓ Students and desk-based workers
✓ Home fitness users
✓ Anyone seeking interactive wellness

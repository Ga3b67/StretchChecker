PoseSense

An interactive pose-checking experience that helps people stay active, improve posture, and move with confidence.


The Problem

Many people want to stretch and exercise but are unsure whether they are performing poses correctly. Without feedback, posture mistakes can reduce effectiveness and discourage consistency.

This project provides real-time pose recognition through a browser-based experience, helping users understand their movements and learn safely.

Who Benefits

✓ Beginners learning proper posture
✓ Students and desk-based workers
✓ People exercising at home
✓ Anyone seeking a fun, interactive workout
